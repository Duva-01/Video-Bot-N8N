Privacy Policy

This page explains the data processed by the Facts Engine dashboard.

What we process

The service stores workflow telemetry, publishing metadata, audit logs and content artifacts required to operate the automation pipeline.

Third-party services

The system may exchange data with Render, Neon, Cloudinary, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok and other providers required for video generation and publishing.

Credentials

Platform credentials and access tokens are used only to operate the connected publishing workflows.

Retention

Logs and metadata may be retained for observability, debugging and manual publishing fallback.
